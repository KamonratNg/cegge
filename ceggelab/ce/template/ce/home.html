CHULALONGKORN UNIVERSITY, BANGKOK, THAILAND

Centre of Excellence in Geotechnical and Geoenvironmental Engineering (CEGGE) at Chulalongkorn University was started in early 2016. The research activities are in the areas of geotechnical and geoenvironmental engineering including constitutive modeling for geomaterial, stress-strain characteristic of soils, numerical analysis in geomechanics, pavement engineering, ground improvement, geotechnical earthquake engineering and soil bioengineering.

Focus Areas

  • Numerical and analytical methods in geomechanics
  • and underground excavation
  • Unsaturated/Saturated constitutive soil models
  • Soil dynamics and geotechnical earthquake engineering
  • Ground improvement and soil stabilisation
  • Rainfall induced landslide and slope stability analysis
  • Reliability analysis in geotechnical engineering
  • Pavement engineering and asphalt technology
  • Construction and building materials
  • Ground contamination and remediation
  • Geophysics and underground survey
